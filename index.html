How To Make
Mac & Cheese

A very quick and easy fix to side-dish.
This recipe is cheap and tasty.

Ingredients:

  • Macaroni
  • Shredded cheese
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Salt

Directions:

First, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boils.


Second, cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes. Then drain.


Third, melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in flour until smooth, about 5 minutes.


After that, slowly pour milk into butter-flour mixture while continuously stirring until mixture is smooth and bubbling, about 5 minutes.


Add Cheddar cheese to milk mixture and stir until cheese is melted, 2 to 4 minutes.


Then, fold macaroni into cheese sauce until coated.


And doneee! mac and cheese ready to serve.
